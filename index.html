Our Atelier

Born from a
Devotion to Cacao

Cocoa Bliss was founded in 2018 by pastry chef Vaibhavi Kharat, trained at Le Cordon Bleu Paris and Valrhona's École du Grand Chocolat. Her dream: to bring the quiet luxury of a Parisian chocolatier to Mumbai's streets.

We source only single-origin cacao — Ecuadorian Arriba Nacional for dark, Belgian Callebaut for milk, and Peruvian Chuncho for our signature blends. Every creation is hand-tempered, hand-finished, and made to order.

Our AI-inspired dessert line bridges culinary tradition with computational creativity — flavour profiles designed through machine learning, executed entirely by human hands.
